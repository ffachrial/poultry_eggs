# Product Context

## Why This Project Exists
The Poultry Eggs project aims to modernize poultry farm management by providing a digital platform for tracking and analyzing egg production, inventory, and related farm operations. Traditional paper-based or spreadsheet systems are inefficient and prone to errors.

## Problems It Solves
- Manual record keeping that's time-consuming and error-prone
- Lack of real-time visibility into egg production metrics
- Difficulty in generating reports for business decisions
- Inefficient inventory management leading to waste or shortages
- Poor traceability of eggs from production to sale

## How It Should Work
- Users can log daily egg production from different poultry houses
- Track feed consumption, mortality rates, and other key metrics
- Monitor inventory levels of eggs, feed, and supplies
- Generate production reports and analytics dashboards
- Set alerts for low inventory or abnormal production patterns
- Support multiple user roles with appropriate permissions

## User Experience Goals
- Intuitive interface requiring minimal training
- Mobile-friendly for use in farm environments
- Quick data entry with validation to prevent errors
- Clear visualizations of key performance indicators
- Fast loading times even with large datasets
- Accessible design following WCAG guidelines
